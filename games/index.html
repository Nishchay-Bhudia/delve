Hanuman's Leap Playable now

A first-person clearing in the forest — three shadow-creatures are loose. Walk up close, hold still, and watch. Built in real C#, compiled to WebAssembly, running entirely in your browser.

⌨️ WASD / arrows to move 🖱️ Mouse to look around ␣ Space + stand still to watch a creature
🌿 For the grown-ups

This is the shad-ripu lesson made physical: there is no combat mechanic because the story doesn't have one either. "Watch it, it settles" is the only tool that works — same as at your dinner table.
