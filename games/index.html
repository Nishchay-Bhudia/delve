Hanuman's Leap Playable now

A side-scrolling platformer through the clearing — jump, swing the gada, clear rocks and shadow-imps blocking the way. Three named creatures wait along the path too, and the mace does nothing to them on purpose.

⌨️ Arrows / A·D to move ⬆️ Space to jump 🏏 X to swing the gada 🙏 E + stand still to watch a named creature
🌿 For the grown-ups

The gada is real combat — smashing generic obstacles is satisfying and that's fine. But Chanchal, Bhaari and Shaan aren't "generic": the mace bounces off them by design, because fighting the actual shad-ripu only makes them bigger. "Watch it, it settles" stays the only tool that works on the three that matter — same as at your dinner table.
