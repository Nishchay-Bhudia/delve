Four Cards

One per episode

😜 Spoilt Brats

What's the biggest "leap" you've made this week without thinking first?

Did it earn a small "curse," like Hanuman's? What was it?

What's one thing you'd think about for one extra second next time?

🏆 The Fierce Competitor

Has anyone ever tried to make you feel small so they'd feel bigger?

Have you ever done that to someone else, even a little?

What happened to Bhim when Duryodhan pushed him down?

💎 The Snobbish Girl

Would your friends still gather round if you had nothing new to show?

What's something about you that has nothing to do with "stuff"?

Who's someone you'd stick with even if their "shine" wore off?

🌱 The Timid Boy

What does your own "battlefield" look like — a test, a match, a stage?

Who is your Krishna — who steadies you when your hands shake?

What's one small thing that helps you breathe before a hard moment?

🌿 For the grown-ups

You don't need all the answers — the question landing is the win. If a card falls flat, skip it; there's no wrong way to have five minutes of real conversation.
