Man Base Re Draft

"Settle, mind, settle." A short, repeatable call-and-response — easy enough for a five-year-old to sing back after one listen. This is a placeholder set to an original tune; the brief leaves open whether dhuns end up original or drawn from the existing kirtan corpus — that call sits with whoever owns the music direction.

Man Base Re — draft take
No recording uploaded yet — player is wired up and ready.

🎵 Lyrics (call and response)

Call: Man base re, man base re…
Response: Hanuman se seekh le, man base re!

Call: Storm ho ya fog ho…
Response: Dekh le, dekh le, man base re!

(Mind, settle down. Learn it from Hanuman, mind, settle down. Storm or fog — just watch it, watch it, mind settles down.)

🌿 For the grown-ups

Sing it in the car. It's short on purpose — a dhun a five-year-old can hold onto is worth more than one only the choir can sing.
