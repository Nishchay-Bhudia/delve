The Story

A village. Three friends.
And a visitor from the ages!

Far from the hustle of the city, in a village in rural India, three friends love a bit of adventure. They are no different from any other kids you or I know — full of games, giggles… and trouble.

A timid boy whose mind never lets him forget. A whirlwind of mischief who once nearly burnt down the science lab. A clever girl who knows the price of everything — and makes sure everyone else does too.

Then one stormy evening, deep in the forest, Hanuman steps out of the darkness with a scrapbook strapped to his back. When he opens it, the pages burst into light — and for the first time, the three friends see their own minds, standing right beside them!

📖 “The scrapbook doesn't show you stories.
It shows you yourself.” — Hanuman

The Guide

The monkey who mastered the monkey mind

Ever heard a busy, jumpy brain called a monkey mind? Well — the guide of this journey is Hanuman: the monkey who became the most focused, brave and devoted being in all the epics.

Once he was the naughtiest kid of all — mighty enough to leap for the sun, thinking it was a gigantic fruit! He knows exactly what it's like to have great powers and not know how to use them yet.

“You have a monkey mind? Wonderful. So do I.
Let me show you what a monkey can do.”

The Big Idea

You are not your mind.
You have one!

In Delve, every mind is a creature — one only you can see. It's not your enemy. It's your superpower-in-training. And it follows three very old rules:

1

Feed it… it grows wild! 🍭

Every "just one more!" makes the creature bigger and bolder.

2

Fight it… it grows wilder! ⛓️

Chain the monkey, and you'll meet a much BIGGER monkey.

3

Watch it… it settles. 🪷

Sit quietly and just watch — and the creature slows down, circles, and sits beside you. The oldest secret in the world.

The Episodes

Every story, a mirror

Each adventure pairs a struggle from the kids' world with a story that has lasted through the ages — and a truth about the mind that connects them both.

Episode 01

Spoilt Brats 😜

All three kids push their mischief too far at home and at school — and life pushes back.

📖 From the Scrapbook

Little Hanuman leaps to swallow the sun itself, thinking it's a gigantic fruit — until his mischief earns him a curse that makes him forget his powers!

The truth: Mischief is part of being a kid — but every action has consequences.

Episode 02

The Fierce Competitor 🏆

Desperate to be the best, Tej secretly sabotages his rival's training — again and again.

📖 From the Scrapbook

Duryodhan, burning with rivalry, goes as far as drowning Bhim in the river. But Bhim returns — stronger than ever before.

The truth: Put hurdles in someone's way, and you only make them stronger than you.

Episode 03

The Snobbish Girl 💎

Mansi's crowd gathers around her latest gadgets — until something shinier appears elsewhere. So do her friends.

📖 From the Scrapbook

The story of the ring, and Ghanshyam Maharaj — people gather around wealth, and scatter the moment they find something better.

The truth: Fame and fortune pull a crowd that leaves when it finds something better.

Episode 04

The Timid Boy 🌱

Mohan's mind is a storm — flashbacks of bullies behind him, impossible dreams ahead — and the present keeps slipping away.

📖 From the Scrapbook

The greatest warrior of the age stands trembling on the battlefield, bow slipping from his hand — until his charioteer teaches him to steady the mind.

The truth: A steady mind is the greatest strength of all.
